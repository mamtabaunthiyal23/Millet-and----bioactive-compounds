Places / locality :-

In India Karnataka is the state in which fingermillet is grown in large amount . Bangalore, Kolar, Tumkur and Hassan are districts of Karnataka which grows Ragi . In Tamil Nadu, it is grown in Salem, Coimbatore, North Arcot, Ramanathapuram, Changalpat and South Arcot districts accounting for nearly 80 per cent of production. Visakhapatnam, Vijayanagaram, Chittoor, Anantapur and Nellore districts of Andhra Pradesh are well known for fingermillet cultivation. Ragi is also grown in Gujarat, West Bengal, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh and Kashmir, rajasthan .
