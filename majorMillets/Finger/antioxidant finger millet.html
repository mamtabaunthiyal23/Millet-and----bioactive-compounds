Antioxidant Properties

Antioxidants are derived from plants that may prevent cancer and aging . Finger millet is enriched with phytochemicals and polyphenols like phenolic acids and tannins are found in the seed coat of finger millet [1] . Finger millet showed highest total flavonoid content in among defatted meals . Polyphenols suppress excessive oxidation responsible for cancer and aging thus, any compound which can neutralize these free radicals can in turn exhibit antiaging effect [2] .Prashant S. Hegde, Namakkal S. Rajasekaran and T.S. Chandra studied the effects of the antioxidant properties of millets on oxidative stress and glycemic status in alloxan-induced rats , therefore the beneficial role of a millet-based diet in protecting against oxidative stress and maintaining glucose levels in vivo in type II diabetes was investigated [3] .The rats fed with finger millet enriched diet because with KM-enriched diet showed a greater reduction in blood glucose and cholesterol . Antioxidant compounds like phenolic acids, polyphenols and flavonoids inhibit the oxidative mechanisms and antioxidants reduce the risk of cardiovascular diseases. Jayanetti Koralalage Ramani Radhika Samarasekera et.al (2021 ) studied anti-inflammatory properties in terms of A5-LOX, XO, hyaluronidase, and oxidative burst inhibitory activitie and antioxidant Properties using different antioxidant assays of Finger Millet (Eleusine coracana (L.) Gaertn.) Varieties Cultivated in Sri Lanka [5] . Phenolic acids from finger millet (Eleusine coracana) milled fractions were isolated and their antioxidant and antimicrobial properties were evaluated and the results indicate that potential exists to utilise finger millet seed coat as an alternative natural antioxidant and food preservative [6] .
