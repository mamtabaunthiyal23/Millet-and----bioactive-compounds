Nutrition

Little millets are a highly nutritious, healthy, and versatile grain that can make a valuable addition to any diet. By creating value-added products using little millets, we can increase income for millet farmers, improve nutrition in rural India, and combat malnutrition, which is a growing concern worldwide due to modern lifestyles that rely heavily on processed fast food and evolving dietary habits. To address this issue, we must develop food products that are rich in nutrients, readily accepted, and cost-effective for growth and development. By using little millets to create various value-added, therapeutic, and functional products in addition to traditional food preparations, we can help increase demand for this grain and reverse the current decline in production caused by underutilization.
